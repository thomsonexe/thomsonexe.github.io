// Who am I?

Hey, I'm Ben — a Junior Security Analyst with a background in Computer Forensics & Security. I work primarily on the blue team side of things, focusing on threat detection, incident response, and vulnerability management day to day.

I hold a few certifications — CompTIA Network+, Security+, and BTL1 — and I'm currently working towards CompTIA SecurityX. In my role I work across tools like Microsoft Sentinel, Defender for Endpoint, Cortex XDR, and Rapid7 InsightVM.

Outside of work I play CTFs, mess around with labs, and occasionally post walkthroughs on my YouTube channel.

// What is this site?

This is my personal corner of the internet. Somewhere to document what I'm learning, share writeups, post notes on tools I'm using at work, and generally keep a record of the things I find interesting in security.

It's not meant to be polished or professional — it's just honest content from someone working in the field and constantly trying to improve.

The site is built entirely in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no CMS.

// What I plan to post

I'm not going to commit to a schedule because I'll immediately break it, but here's roughly what you can expect:

  • Lab writeups — CTF challenges, TryHackMe rooms, CyberDefenders scenarios. Mostly blue team focused.
  • Blog posts — notes on tools I'm using at work, concepts I'm learning, or things I think are worth writing down.
  • YouTube — CTF walkthroughs in video format on my channel.

If something on here is useful to you, great. If not, that's fine too — I'm writing it for myself as much as anyone else.

— ben
